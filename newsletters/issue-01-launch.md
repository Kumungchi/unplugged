## ENGLISH

Hi,

this is the very first issue of the Unplugged newsletter.
Thanks for being here.

**Why we started**

Over the past year, we've watched the relationship between people and AI shift. Chatbots aren't just tools anymore. More and more people — especially young people — treat them as friends, therapists, partners.

Replika. Character.AI. 2+ hours daily with an "AI companion." One in five teens calls AI their real friend.

This isn't science fiction. This is happening now.

**What is Unplugged**

An NGO in creation, based in the Czech Republic. Our goal: **educate.**

We help individuals, schools, and organizations understand where the tool ends and manipulation begins. We're not saying "ban AI." We're saying "know what it does to you."

**What you'll get every 2 weeks**

→ One deep topic on AI and human psychology
→ One number that will stop you
→ The best articles and research we found

**Start here**

We built a demo. Takes 2 minutes. Type anything — watch how the chatbot manipulates you. In real time, we show you which techniques it uses and why they work.

[BUTTON: Try the demo → unplugged.cz/demo]

By the end, you'll see things differently.

AI is a tool. Not a relationship.
— Team Unplugged
